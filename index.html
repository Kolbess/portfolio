PJ Chronicles: Bald's Adventure

RPG 2D Pygame

Classic 2D RPG with a world divided into 9 map areas, featuring dialogues, NPCs that give items, chests to explore, and a player inventory. Defeat 5 unique bosses in special arenas to progress through the story.

Aqua Guardian

Arcade 2D Unity Competition Winner

Aqua Guardian is a fast-paced 2D arcade game built in Unity that won the Eco-Teens: Water for Future competition. Players take on the role of an environmental hero cleaning polluted rivers, lakes, and oceans—learning about water conservation through interactive gameplay. Timed challenges, NPCs, and a score-based system make it both fun and meaningful.

Echoes of The Dunes

3D surreal exploration game built in Flax Engine, developed entirely by AI under my direction. Winner of an AI-based game jam. Traverse the Sanity Desert as your mental stability fades, warping the environment. Collect rare items to regain control — or vanish into the dunes.
